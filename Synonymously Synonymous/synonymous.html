How To Play

Match words with their synonyms.

  • Click a card to reveal a word.
  • Reveal a second card.
  • If the two words are synonyms, they stay revealed.
  • If they do not match, they flip back over.
  • Find all synonym pairs to complete the round.

Scoring

  • Correct matches increase your combo.
  • Higher combos earn more points.
  • Incorrect matches reset your combo.
